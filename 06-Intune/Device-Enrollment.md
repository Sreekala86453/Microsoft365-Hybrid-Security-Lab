# Device Enrollment

## Overview

Microsoft Intune was prepared to manage Windows devices within the Microsoft 365 tenant.

During this phase of the project, no Windows devices were enrolled. The focus was on establishing the management infrastructure required for future endpoint onboarding.

---

## Device Group

| Group | Purpose |
|---------|----------|
| Windows Managed Devices | Target group for Intune policy assignments |

---

## Enrollment Status

No Windows devices were enrolled during this phase.

Future phases of this project will include:

- Windows 11 VM enrollment
- Microsoft Entra Join
- Automatic MDM Enrollment
- Device Compliance Validation

---

## Business Justification

Preparing Intune before device enrollment reflects enterprise deployment practices. Policies and security baselines are configured first, allowing newly enrolled devices to receive configurations automatically.
