## Possible Issues may encounter and the troubleshooting steps

## Issue 1 – Unable to access Microsoft 365 Admin Center

### Symptoms

The Microsoft 365 Admin Center cannot be accessed after signing in.

### Possible Causes

- Incorrect account
- Missing Global Administrator permissions
- Browser cache
- Service outage

### Resolution

- Verify that you are signing in with the Global Administrator account.
- Confirm the correct tenant is selected.
- Clear browser cache or use an InPrivate/Incognito window.
- Check the Microsoft 365 Service Health dashboard.

---

## Issue 2 – Subscription not displayed

### Symptoms

No Microsoft 365 subscription appears under **Billing > Your products**.

### Possible Causes

- Trial provisioning is still in progress.
- Incorrect tenant selected.

### Resolution

- Wait 15–30 minutes after creating the tenant.
- Verify the correct directory is selected.
- Refresh the portal.

---

## Issue 3 – Licenses unavailable

### Symptoms

Unable to assign Microsoft 365 licenses.

### Possible Causes

- No licenses available
- Incorrect administrator role

### Resolution

- Verify available licenses under **Billing > Licenses**.
- Ensure you have Global Administrator or License Administrator permissions.
